iDentity

Resources for Hampstead School students learning HTML and CSS at the British Museum Samsung Centre

The MuseumXDesign Project is centred around identity and how it connects to the past.

On the first day, we brainstormed on what identity means and how we identify ourselves.

On the second we brought in the item we consider the most important to us. I chose my ipad. We looked around the Korea and had to choose an item that related to our own object. I chose ancient Korean coins. We then took photos of both objects and of ourselves.

On the third day, we used photoshop to create an image/poster, inocrperting our phtography into it.

It was facinating to do this because you never really stop and consider how technology of today can relate to something from an Ancient Era.
